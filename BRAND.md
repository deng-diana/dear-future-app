# REUNITE — Brand Identity v1.0

## Brand Name

**Reunite**

---

## Brand Meaning

Reunite is not about writing a letter.

It is about meeting a version of yourself that time almost erased.

Years later, when life has changed and memories have faded, Reunite brings back a forgotten moment, a forgotten feeling, and a forgotten self.

It is a reunion across time.

---

## Core Tagline

**Meet the person you used to be.**

---

## Alternative Taglines

- A reunion with your past self.
- Some things deserve to return.
- Preserved by time.
- See you again, someday.

---

## Brand Positioning

Reunite is a digital time capsule.

- Not a productivity tool.
- Not a journal.
- Not an email service.

It is a quiet place where people preserve a moment of their lives and rediscover it years later.

---

## Emotional Keywords

Reunion · Time · Memory · Reflection · Growth · Nostalgia · Hope · Humanity · Warmth · Legacy

---

## Brand Personality

Quiet · Elegant · Timeless · Thoughtful · Human · Crafted · Emotional · Intentional

- Never playful.
- Never noisy.
- Never productivity-focused.
- Never AI-looking.

---

## Logo Concept

**Primary Logo:** Wax Seal + North Star

**Symbolism:**

- The wax seal represents preservation.
- The star represents guidance through time.

Together they symbolize: *“A moment preserved today, rediscovered tomorrow.”*

---

## Logo Style

- Circular wax seal.
- Deep Bordeaux red seal.
- Gold North Star in the center.
- Subtle orbital dots around the star.
- No envelope in the primary mark.
- The envelope can appear in illustrations but not in the master logo.

**Reason:**

- The envelope represents a letter.
- The seal represents a ritual.
- The ritual is more unique than the letter.

---

## Logo Usage

- **Primary:** Seal + Reunite wordmark
- **Secondary:** Seal only
- **App Icon:** Seal only
- **Favicon:** Star only

---

---

## Typography

- **Display / Headlines:** Cormorant Garamond SemiBold — *elegant, literary, timeless, human.*
- **Body:** Cormorant Garamond Regular
- **Metadata / Time:** IBM Plex Mono Medium — *archive, timestamp, time capsule, documentary feeling.*

---

## Design Principles

- 90% whitespace
- 5% texture
- 5% details

Never overdesign. Every element should feel intentional.

---

## UI Style

Paper-first interface. The screen should feel like paper, not software.

- No cards.
- No dashboards.
- No glassmorphism.
- No gradients.
- No AI aesthetics.
- No productivity patterns.

Users should feel: *“I am writing a letter, not using an app.”*

## Splash Screen

## Product Philosophy

People don’t need another app.

People need a chance to meet themselves again.

That is the purpose of Reunite.

- Not memory.
- Not storage.
- Not productivity.

**Reunion.**
