Common questions

Where did my letter go after I sealed it?
That is on purpose. The moment you seal a letter it leaves the app for good — no list, no inbox, no copy you can re-open. It returns to you as an email on the future day you chose.

When will my letter arrive?
On the exact delivery day you picked, it arrives in your email inbox. The full text always lives in your own inbox, so it outlives the app.

I want to delete my account.
You can delete your account from inside the app. Deleting your account cancels any letters that have not yet been delivered.

Something is broken, or I have another question.
Email the address above and tell us what happened. The more detail, the faster we can help.
